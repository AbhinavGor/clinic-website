Dr.TS Muthu

MBBS, MD(Gen Med), DM(Neuro)

About the doctor

Dr. Muthu is an Assistant Professor in Neurology at Government Vellore Medical College & Hospital in Vellore. He is available for private consultation at Sri Ragav Neuro Clinic located on Kosa Street, and is also associated with Sri Narayani Hospital. Dr. Muthu treats disorders of the brain, spinal cord, nerves and muscles. These include epilepsy, headache, giddiness, paralysis, memory loss, tremors, back and neck pain, and abnormal movements. He did his postgraduate, superspeciality education and training at the Institute of Neurology, Madras Medical College, Chennai.

Prior to his current assignment, Dr. Muthu served as Assistant Professor at the Institute of Neurology of Madras Medical College in Chennai, and as Assistant Professor at the Institute of Child Health, also in Chennai. He has 23 yearsâ€™ overall experience in medical practice, of which the last seven (2009-2019) have been in the Neurology speciality.

Dr. Muthu attends to outpatients and inpatients suffering from neurological illnesses, including those requiring neurological intensive care. He is also involved in teaching undergraduate and postgraduate medical students and students of physiotherapy and nursing. Dr. Muthu has presented a number of papers and posters at national medical conferences. He enjoys writing articles on health-related topics for the general public and conducting educational programmes for medical professionals. His priorities are excellent patient care and cost-effective treatment. Dr. Muthu wishes to treat and help as many patients as possible during his medical career.
